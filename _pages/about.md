<h4>你好，我是 Kyle</h4>

<p>我從清大念材料、到史丹佛做固態電池研究，畢業後加入矽谷的新創從零開發 AI 電池管理系統，現在在 TikTok 廣告部門當 MLOps Engineer，負責廣告推薦系統背後的大規模 data pipelines 的穩定性。這個 blog 是我把走過的路、學到的事、看過的書，想清楚後寫下來的地方。</p>

<p>我想寫的主題：</p>

<ul>
  <li>新創 vs. 大公司 — 職涯轉型，以及兩種環境真正教會你的事</li>
  <li>從 AI Infra 角度看 AI 應用</li>
  <li>自我成長：書本框架和理論放到真實生活裡的檢驗</li>
  <li>台灣到矽谷的冒險之路</li>
</ul>

<p>寫這個部落格是因為寫作會逼我誠實面對「我到底懂多少」。如果這裡的某篇文章能讓你也獲得些甚麼，那就更棒了。</p>

<h5>背景</h5>

<p>目前在 TikTok 廣告部門擔任 MLOps Engineer SRE，負責全球大規模即時資料系統的可靠性工程，確保廣告推薦模型能持續即時更新。在此之前任職於 Element Energy（矽谷 Series B 新創），負責電池管理系統的 ML 模型與資料基礎建設。</p>

<p>史丹佛大學材料科學碩士（William Chueh 教授實驗室，固態電池小組）；國立清華大學材料科學學士＋經濟學學士（蔡哲瑋教授實驗室，研究高熵合金）。發表七篇論文，被引用 300+ 次 — <a href="https://scholar.google.com.tw/citations?user=itnDHV0AAAAJ" target="_blank">Google Scholar</a>。</p>

<p>北美台灣工程師協會（NATEA）矽谷分會理事，史丹佛台灣學生會前任會長。</p>

<p>下班後：跑步、攝影、吉他、看書、學習。</p>

<h4>Hi, I'm Kyle</h4>

<p>I went from researching solid-state batteries at Stanford, to building AI battery management systems at a Series B startup, to owning reliability for PB-scale data systems at TikTok Ads. This blog is where I write about what I learn along the way — usually a few months after I've stopped feeling clueless about it.</p>

<p>Topics I'm drawn to:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Startup vs. big tech — career pivots and what each environment actually teaches you</li>
  <li>Applied AI from the infrastructure side</li>
  <li>Self-development — frameworks from books, tested against real life</li>
  <li>The Taiwan-to-Silicon Valley adventure</li>
</ul>

<p>I write because writing forces me to be honest about what I actually understand. If a post helps you learn something, even better.</p>

<h5>Background</h5>

<p>Currently MLOps Engineer at TikTok Ads, where I own reliability for the real-time data systems that keep ads recommendation models training continuously. Previously at Element Energy, building ML and data infrastructure for battery management systems.</p>

<p>MS in Materials Science from Stanford (solid-state batteries, Chueh Lab). BS in Materials Science + BA in Economics from National Tsing Hua University (high-entropy alloys, Professor Che-Wei Tsai's lab). Seven papers, 300+ citations — <a href="https://scholar.google.com.tw/citations?user=itnDHV0AAAAJ" target="_blank">Google Scholar</a>.</p>

<p>Board member at NATEA Silicon Valley. Former President of the Stanford Taiwanese Student Association.</p>

<p>Off-hours: running, photography, guitar, reading, and learning.</p>

<p>If something here resonates, or you're working on something interesting in AI or startups — I'd like to hear from you.</p>
